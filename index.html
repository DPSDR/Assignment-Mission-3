The perfect venue for your next event

From large galas to small banquets, Sweet Convention Center is serving for more than 5 years with the superiority and reputation. We take the pride of being the venue of choice for those special events. Till now we have the glory to organize more than 2000 matrimonial events.

Atmosphere and Decor

A wide and vast view with the green environment, Sweet Convention Center is the best place for Wedding, Reception and Holud night. This venue is totally customizable and facilitated with all modern amenities for making your ceremony impressive and memorable.

Facilities and Capacities

Venue facilities: Two (02) Green Rooms, Central Air Condition (only for Halls), CC TV Camera Coverage, Free Wi-Fi, Hygienic Fresh Room, One (01) Archway Gate, Two (02) Microphones, One (01) Raffle Draw Box.

Venue Direction

In spite of being one of the largest conventions, it's very easy to reach your destination hall. You can look at the venue map and where to park your car on this directional map. In addition, you can take support from our highly trained security personnel for any kind of query.
